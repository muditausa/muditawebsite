November 25, 2023

Union: A Cultural Narrative presented at the Orlando Rental Studio

Join us for an unique evening of cross-cultural collaboration featuring music and dance of multiple cultures! Mudita America brings four prominent artists of today to Orlando who will each bring their unique artistic practice for this merger. Marin Naruse: a Japanese traditional singer and Sanshin player (uta-sha) from Amami Oshima Island, Japan; Eric Rokni: a cellist of Jewish and Persian upbringing; Tanuja Krishna: Indian Classical dancer; and Marcinko Orozco, a hip-hip influenced keyboard player. Their work on this project will showcase an extraordinary story of oppression and unification.
